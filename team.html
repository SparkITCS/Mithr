Team Mithr

Mithr Foundation is an association of like minded people who believe in contributing to student empowerment by providing an opportunity to pursue education according to merit.
Scholarships are offered by the members of Mithr Foundation as part of their social commitment and responsibility.

Become a Member?

Thank you for showing interest in Mithr Foundation. You can join your hands with us as a volunteer of Mithr as well as a donor. It doesn't matter the volume of amount you donate. Even with your hundred rupee note, we convert it in the form of note books, pen, files etc. The difference is every penny you donate will reach to a deserving student.
